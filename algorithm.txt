PROBLEM: Write an algorithm that reads a sentence and determine;
   [a] Length of the sentence
   [b] Number of words in the sentence
   [c] Number of vowels in the sentence

Create a varialbe holding:
    sentence, wordCount, vowelChar, vowelCount

SENTENCE: Read the sentence

On getting to a space add 1 to wordCount

Continue counting until you get to point

Read vowelChar

Read sentence char by char

if sentence match any vowelChar

Add 1 to vowelCount
